# Create Villager Labor

[**English**](#english) | [**中文**](#中文)

## English

### Human-Centric Automation for Create

This mod aims to bring a touch of lively, handcrafted warmth to your factories, assembly lines, and all kinds of machinery. Instead of cold, lifeless industrial steel, you can have Villagers, TLM maids, or even yourself sit right at the workstation and personally tend to the production process.

The mod includes a full set of recipes (though balance may be a bit rough) and is fully playable in survival mode.

### Features

- **5 Workstation Seats**: Press Seat, Mixer Seat, Saw Seat, Millstone Seat, Deployer Seat
- **Worker Assignment**: Assign Villagers, Touhou Little Maid maids, or sit on the seat yourself
- **Full Recipe Support**: Compatible with Create's recipes including sequencing assembly, cutting, pressing, milling, and deploying
- **Basin Integration**: Mixer Seat can interact directly with Create's Basin
- **Dyeable**: Color your seats with any of the 16 dye colors
- **Tool Durability**: Deployer Seat consumes tool durability instead of the tool itself

### Usage

1. Craft any workstation seat
2. Hold Shift and right-click a Villager (or maid) with the **Villager Binder**
3. Hold Shift and right-click the seat with the **Villager Binder** to assign the worker
4. Place the seat adjacent to a Belt, Depot, or Basin and provide a valid recipe input

### Notes

- For balance and manual labor efficiency, processing speed is intentionally low
- Belt speeds above 16 RPM may occasionally cause item loss during processing — this is **by design**

## 中文

### 机械动力：村民劳工 —— 以人为本

本 mod 致力于为您的工厂、流水线及各样机械增添更加手动的烟火气。

比起工业、钢铁的死气沉沉，可以让村民、车万女仆甚至由玩家亲自坐在工位前，亲手参与生产流程。

本模组加入了完整的配方（尽管平衡性可能稍显不足），但支持生存模式游玩。

### 特性

- **5 种工位座椅**：冲压工位、搅拌工位、切割工位、研磨工位、组合工位
- **劳工指派**：可指派村民、车万女仆，或玩家亲自上阵
- **完整配方支持**：兼容机械动力的配方，包括序列组装、切割、冲压、研磨、组合等
- **工作盆联动**：搅拌工位可直接与机械动力的工作盆（Basin）交互
- **可染色**：支持 16 种染料颜色染色
- **工具耐久**：组合工位消耗工具耐久而非直接消耗工具

### 使用方法

1. 制作任意工位座椅
2. 手持**村民选择器**，按住 Shift 右键村民（或女仆）
3. 手持**村民选择器**，按住 Shift 右键座椅即可指派劳工
4. 将座椅放置在传送带、置物台或工作盆旁，并提供有效的配方输入

### 注意事项

- 基于平衡性和人工效率的考量，本模组的处理速度相对较低
- 超过 16rpm 时生产过程中偶尔会出现漏件的情况（**这是有意为之**）
